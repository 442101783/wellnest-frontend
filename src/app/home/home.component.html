WellNest

Encapsulating the fusion of 'Nest' and 'Wellness,' embodying a comforting and health-focused ethos. The term 'Nest' conjures a sense of security and care, while 'Wellness' signifies a commitment to holistic health.

Family

Focused on providing comprehensive healthcare services tailored to the needs of families. Offering medical care for individuals of all ages, from infants to seniors, with an emphasis on preventive care, health promotion, and disease management within the family context.

Services provided by the Family Department often include routine check-ups, vaccinations, management of chronic conditions, women's health services including prenatal care and family planning, pediatric care, geriatric care, and counseling on lifestyle and behavioral changes to improve overall family health.

The Family Department serves as a primary point of contact for families seeking medical care and strives to establish long-term relationships with patients, offering continuity of care and personalized treatment plans to address the unique health concerns and challenges faced by each family member. The department may also collaborate closely with other specialties within the clinic to ensure comprehensive and coordinated care for families.

Cardiology

Specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the heart and circulatory system. Staffed by highly trained cardiologists, nurses, and support staff, this department offers a wide range of services to patients experiencing cardiovascular conditions, ranging from routine check-ups to advanced diagnostic procedures and treatments.

Cardiologists within the department work closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans, which may include lifestyle modifications, medication management, interventional procedures, or surgical interventions. The department also emphasizes preventive care, providing education on risk factor reduction, healthy lifestyle choices, and regular screenings to help patients maintain optimal heart health.

Overall, the Cardiology Department plays a crucial role in promoting cardiovascular wellness, diagnosing and managing heart conditions, and improving the quality of life for patients affected by heart disease.

General

Serves as a fundamental unit providing primary healthcare services to patients of all ages and backgrounds. It serves as the initial point of contact for individuals seeking medical care and offers a wide range of essential healthcare services aimed at addressing common health concerns, managing acute illnesses, and promoting overall wellness.

Staffed by general practitioners, nurses, and support staff, the General Department provides comprehensive medical care, including routine check-ups, preventive screenings, vaccinations, diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses and injuries, and management of chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma.

The department emphasizes continuity of care, building long-term relationships with patients, and addressing their healthcare needs holistically. It often serves as a gateway to specialized care, referring patients to other departments or specialists within the clinic when necessary.
